About hospital

Indraprastha Hospitals, New Delhi is the first hospital in India to be Internationally Accredited by Joint Commission International (JCI) consecutively for the fifth time. It is one of the best multi-speciality tertiary acute care hospitals ranked No. 1 in leading surveys with over 700 beds in India and the most sought after destination in the SAARC region for healthcare delivery. Indraprastha Hospital is a state-of-the art modern facility located in the heart of the capital. Currently the hospital has 718 beds with a capacity to expand to 1000 beds. Right from the infrastructure to the latest medical technology acquisition, Indraprastha Hospitals has always kept its patients first and strived to deliver not world class but the world’s best care to its patients since its inception in 1996
